Q) What is a "SONIC BOOM"?

Ans) When an Object Moves in air with a speed greater than the speed of sound in air, it is said to be travelling in Super Sonic Speed.

For Example

Jet Fighters

Gun Bullets

Space Shuttles

Bombs Of Hiroshima And Nagasaki



Due to the Motion of the Object with Supersonic Speed, Shock waves are produced in air. These shockwaves carry a great amount of energy and travel with speed of sound These Waves consist a very high pressure layer followed by a lower pressure layer producing a burst or cracking sound called 'Sonic Boom'.
